The Best Movies According to Noah

My top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Interstellar

The concept is really interesting and unique. The visuals reinforce the idea of the film's uniqueness

Manchester By the Sea

This movie was very sad, and had similarly themed music and visuals. Very effective in it's pacing.

Nausicaa

Even though this movie was similar to Princess Mononoke, I thought that the visuals were so cool for the 70s, and was a clear inspiration for the movies which came afterwards.
